《最后生还者》车站关卡拆分

发表于2024-03-17|更新于2024-10-24|游戏设计

原视频链接：The Last of Us - Level Design Breakdown

笔记

The Orientation of the Level

  • Narrative:
    • Joel：犹豫、担心、接受过去
    • Ellie：犹豫、成长、决心

Beats

High Way

  • Ellie’s AI: Hang out back form the player, drag feet – something weighing on her mind, to show her complex emotion in the end of the journey.

  • Guide Players to the caravan
    Veiw: the bird flying when walk up to, the blocked road, opened door
    Auido: birds’ noise

Bus Station(Waiting Hall)

  • Seeing Exit at floor 2, puzzle on floor 1 to get on floor 2
  • Break the established rules – Ellie ingores the player’s interaction.
    • A specific thing players expect is ingored – break the estabilsh rule on player, but it has contributed a story beat through game mechanics.
    • It’s has successfully told a story through the systems and mechanics that the player haave at their disposal.

Giraff

  • The appearance of giraff comes after a serise of foreshading(blur glass…), making the final reveal more impactful.
  • Light: The enviroment change form dark → light, narrow → board, highlighting the apperance of giraff.
  • On the balcony, Joel and Ellie are watching giraffs leving, player is the person to stop the moment and carry on with the game.

After Ellie leaves and we can see giraff leave as well.It’s a sympol that something has leaves away form Ellie – her innocent childhood and she knows her responiablity to save human-bing and she has to leave form Joel and the days with him can’t being exit any longer.

café

  • Climax of giraff, Relation on balcaony → café – a period of lenthy,dark upstairs time pulling players back into the normal flow – go on with the journey.
  • Several resources set in café also help player adjusts mood and turns back into the level.

Bus Station

  • Ellie hand over the photo to Joe: There is a branch that player makes decision and trigger different plots.
  • This part accomplishes the orientation that present Joel’s change on his past – Joel takes over the photo and accept the past – but it’s depends on player him/herself which reinforce Player’s sense of substitution of the role of Joel.
  • Narrative on bus station goes with collecting.
